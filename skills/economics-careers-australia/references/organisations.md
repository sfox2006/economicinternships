# Target Organisations — Economics Careers Australia

Comprehensive list of Australian employers running economics internships and graduate programs, grouped by sector. Includes public sector (federal and state), private sector (banks, asset managers, consultancies), think tanks, nonprofits, and international organisations based in Australia.

For each organisation, the relevant programs and their typical cycles are listed. Verify live before drafting.

---

## 🏛️ Public Sector — Federal Government & Regulators

These are the core economics employers in the federal public sector. Most run a single annual Graduate Program and a Summer Vacation/Internship Program. Applications typically open March–April for the Graduate Program (start the following year) and May–July for the Summer Vacation Program (the following Nov–Feb).

### Core economic agencies

- **Department of the Treasury** — Graduate Program (Economics, Tax, Markets streams); Summer Vacation Program; Indigenous Economic Cadetship — https://treasury.gov.au/the-department/careers
- **Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA)** — Graduate Program (Economist and Analyst streams); Cadetship Program (penultimate-year undergrads, part-time during study + full-time over summer); Summer Internship — https://www.rba.gov.au/careers/
- **Productivity Commission** — Graduate Development Program; Cadetship Program; Summer Research Program — https://www.pc.gov.au/about/careers
- **Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS)** — Graduate Program (Economics, Data Analytics, Statistical Programming, Indigenous); ABS Cadetship Program — https://www.abs.gov.au/careers
- **Department of Finance** — Graduate Program (Economics & Financial Management stream); Summer Vacation Program — https://www.finance.gov.au/about-us/careers

### Financial regulators

- **Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA)** — Graduate Program; Industry Placement Program — https://www.apra.gov.au/careers
- **Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)** — Graduate Program; Summer Vacation Program — https://www.asic.gov.au/about-asic/careers-at-asic/
- **Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) & Australian Energy Regulator (AER)** — Graduate Program (Economics stream); Cadetship — https://www.accc.gov.au/about-us/careers
- **Australian Taxation Office (ATO)** — Graduate Program (Economics, Tax, Data streams) — https://www.ato.gov.au/about-ato/careers

### Departments with strong economics work

- **Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT)** — Graduate Program (includes Economist stream); Indigenous Graduate Program — https://www.dfat.gov.au/about-us/careers/graduate-program
- **Department of Industry, Science and Resources** — Graduate Program; Summer Vacation Program — https://www.industry.gov.au/about-us/careers
- **Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water (DCCEEW)** — Graduate Program — https://www.dcceew.gov.au/about/careers
- **Department of Employment and Workplace Relations** — Graduate Program — https://www.dewr.gov.au/about-department/careers
- **Department of Social Services (DSS)** — Graduate Program (Economics & Policy stream) — https://www.dss.gov.au/about-the-department/employment
- **Department of Health and Aged Care** — Graduate Program — https://www.health.gov.au/about-us/employment-and-careers
- **Department of Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development, Communications and the Arts** — Graduate Program — https://www.infrastructure.gov.au/department/about/careers

- **Department of Veterans' Affairs** — Graduate Program — https://www.dva.gov.au/about-us/careers

### Trade, investment, and finance

- **Austrade (Australian Trade and Investment Commission)** — Graduate Program — https://www.austrade.gov.au/about/careers
- **Export Finance Australia** — Graduate Program; Internships — https://www.exportfinance.gov.au/careers/
